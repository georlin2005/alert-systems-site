About ALERT SYSTEMS

Founded in 2010 in Veliyannur, Thrissur, Alert Systems began as a dedicated home automation company offering advanced solutions like CCTV surveillance, remote-controlled gates, and video door phones. With a strong focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we quickly gained the trust of homeowners and businesses across the region.

As demand grew, we expanded our services to include custom gate fabrication, bringing together security and craftsmanship under one roof. Today, with over a decade of industry experience, we continue to deliver end-to-end security solutions — from consultation and professional installation to regular maintenance.

At Alert Systems, your safety is our priority. We combine cutting-edge technology with dependable service to protect what matters most.

Our Vision

At Alert Systems, we envision a Kerala where every home and business is empowered by seamless, intelligent security. We see a future where smart technologies like CCTV surveillance, automated gates, and video door phones are not luxuries — but everyday essentials that offer safety, convenience, and peace of mind for all.

Instead of manufacturing our own devices, we focus on sourcing high-quality systems from trusted brands and turning them into practical, easy-to-use solutions. Through expert installation, integration, and ongoing support, we bridge the gap between modern technology and real-world needs — ensuring every customer experiences the full value of smart security.

Looking ahead, we aim to lead Kerala’s transition into a smart-living era — where technology, craftsmanship, and personal care work together to protect what matters most.

Our Mission

At Alert Systems, our mission is to make smart, secure living a reality for homes and businesses across Thrissur. Through trusted technologies like CCTV, remote-controlled gates, video door phones, and custom gate fabrication, we provide practical solutions tailored to everyday needs.
